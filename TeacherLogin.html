Teacher Account Terms & Conditions

Welcome to our educational platform! Before proceeding with your teacher account registration, please carefully review and accept the following terms and conditions. By clicking “I Accept,” you acknowledge your understanding and commitment to comply with these rules, ensuring a professional and safe learning environment for all participants.

Professional Conduct: As a teacher, you are expected to uphold the highest standards of professionalism. Communication with students, peers, and platform administrators must remain respectful and courteous at all times. Harassment, discriminatory behavior, or inappropriate language is strictly prohibited and may result in account suspension or termination.

Course Material & Content: All materials provided, including documents, videos, and images, must be original or appropriately licensed. Content should be accurate, educational, and suitable for the intended audience. The platform reserves the right to remove any content that violates copyright regulations, contains misinformation, or is deemed inappropriate.

Student Privacy & Data Protection: Teachers are required to safeguard student privacy. Personal information, photos, or recordings obtained during teaching must remain confidential. Sharing sensitive data outside the platform is strictly forbidden. Compliance with applicable privacy laws and platform guidelines is mandatory.

Scheduling & Attendance: Teachers should provide clear schedules for live sessions and ensure punctuality. If unable to attend a session, notify students and platform administrators in advance. Frequent absences or lack of communication may impact account standing and credibility.

Communication & Feedback: Constructive feedback enhances the student learning experience. Maintain professional communication through the platform’s messaging or comment systems. Sharing personal contact details is not permitted unless explicitly authorized by the platform.

Platform Use & Conduct: Your account is strictly for educational purposes. Misuse, including spamming, posting offensive content, or promoting unrelated services, is prohibited. The platform reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts violating these rules.

Payment & Financial Compliance: Teachers handling paid courses must follow platform policies for all financial transactions. Ensure accurate information and compliance with applicable laws when processing course fees or payments.

Policy Updates: The platform may update these terms periodically. Continued use of your account constitutes acceptance of all revised conditions. Staying informed and adhering to updated policies is the responsibility of each teacher.

By clicking “I Accept,” you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to abide by these terms. Your commitment ensures a professional, safe, and high-quality learning experience for every student. We appreciate your dedication to fostering a positive and engaging educational community.
